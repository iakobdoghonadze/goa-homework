an architectural complex in Georgia, Kazbegi municipality, 2200 meters above sea level. Sameba Khevi was the main shrine. The walled complex includes the Domed Cathedral of the Holy Trinity (30s of the 14th century), a bell tower (2nd half of the 14th century) and a bell tower (15th century).
